## **KEY DESIGN PRINCIPLES:**

1. **Clear Visual Hierarchy:** Biggest = most important
2. **Benefit-Driven Copy:** Focus on what the customer gets, not what you do
3. **Multiple CTAs:** Make it easy to contact you at every stage
4. **Social Proof Throughout:** Trust signals everywhere
5. **Mobile-First:** Everything works perfectly on phones
6. **Fast Loading:** Compress images, optimize code
7. **Localized:** IDR pricing, local areas, Indonesian + English
8. **Scannable:** Use bullets, short paragraphs, clear headers

---

## **CONVERSION OPTIMIZATION TIPS:**

**Use "You" language:** 
❌ "We provide excellent service"
✓ "You'll love coming home to a crystal-clear pool"

**Create urgency (subtle):**
- "Limited slots available this month"
- "Join 300+ happy pool owners"
- "20% off - New customers only"

**Remove friction:**
- No required fields except phone/WhatsApp
- Show pricing upfront
- "No contract required"
- "Cancel anytime"

**Build trust immediately:**
- Years in business
- Number of pools serviced
- Real reviews with photos
- Guarantee statements

## Image Editing Guidelines

### **Color Correction:**

- **Exposure:** +10 to +20 (bright and airy)
- **Contrast:** -5 to +10 (soft, not harsh)

- **Shadows:** +15 (lift dark areas)
- **Whites:** +10 (crisp, clean)
- **Saturation:** -5 to 0 (natural, not oversaturated)
- **Blue/Cyan:** +10 to +15 (enhance water color)

### **Filters to Apply:**

- Slight blue tint overlay (5-10% opacity)
- Soft vignette (subtle, not noticeable)
- Sharpening: Medium (for web)
- Clarity: +10 to +15 (crisp details)

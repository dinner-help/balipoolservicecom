Task Overview:
Create a new web section that includes both a WhatsApp-integrated booking system and a Team Showcase area to display the company’s staff by location.

⸻

🧑‍🍳 1. Website “Cooker” Build (Main Functional Section)

Goal:
Design a clean, modern web section that fits seamlessly with the current site’s style and colors.

Features to Include:
	1.	WhatsApp Button (Global CTA):
	•	Add a fixed floating WhatsApp button that appears on all pages.
	•	When clicked, it should open WhatsApp directly with a pre-filled message like:
“Hi! I’d like to book a service / place an order.”
	•	Button should appear clearly on mobile and desktop.
	2.	Auto Chat Pop-Up:
	•	After 25 seconds on the site, a chat bubble slides up with a message like:
“Need help or want to book faster? We’re here to assist you 💬”
	•	The chat offers two choices:
a) Fill in your name + WhatsApp number → triggers a call-back notification to your team.
b) Start a direct WhatsApp chat instantly.
	3.	Booking & Inquiry Form:
	•	Short form: Name, WhatsApp number, and optional message.
	•	Once submitted, it triggers a WhatsApp message or API notification to your sales/support number.
	•	The form should look friendly and load fast.

⸻

👥 2. Team Section Build (The “Best Team Area”)

Goal:
Showcase a professional Indonesian team of 20 people, divided by service areas to make the business look large, organized, and trustworthy.

Team Composition:
	•	Total: 20 team members
	•	Include area-specific groups:
	•	Sanur (2 people + 1 local customer photo)
	•	Canggu (2 people)
	•	Seminyak (2 people)
	•	Uluwatu (2 people)
	•	Nusa Dua (2 people)
	•	Jimbaran (2 people)
	•	Remaining positions: 1 Area Manager, 1 Customer Support, 1 Operations Lead, 1 Marketing, and general staff to reach 20 total.
	•	All should appear as young professional Indonesian men, dressed neatly (polo shirts, uniforms, or branded shirts).

Display Style:
	•	Create a group photo showing all 20 people together, labeled with their city or department (e.g., “Canggu Team,” “Sanur Team,” “Area Manager”).
	•	Underneath, list each name and short description of their role, for example:
“Ari – Sanur Pool Technician | Expert in saltwater systems”
	•	Include icons or small location badges to make the page feel localized.

Optional:
	•	Create a second “Our Team” page (standalone) that links from the footer and includes the full detailed profiles for each area manager and staff member.
	•	Link this team page to related service area pages (Seminyak, Canggu, Uluwatu, etc.) to show connection between team and location.

⸻

🎯 Design & Style Notes
	•	Match current website’s layout, fonts, and button styles.
	•	Use professional pool-related background images and real working shots for credibility.
	•	Keep everything mobile-friendly and SEO-optimized (each area’s team and WhatsApp button should have local relevance).

⸻

✅ Deliverables Summary
	•	One new main section (“Cooker” → booking + WhatsApp integration + chat automation).
	•	One new Team Section with 20 staff across 6 areas.
	•	Optional: One standalone “Team” page for internal linking and SEO.

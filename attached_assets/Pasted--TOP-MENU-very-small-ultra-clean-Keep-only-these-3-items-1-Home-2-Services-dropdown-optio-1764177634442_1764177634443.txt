

TOP MENU (very small, ultra-clean)

Keep only these 3 items:
	1.	Home
	2.	Services (dropdown optional)
	3.	Contact / WhatsApp button

That’s it.
No “About”, no “Gallery”, no “Why us”, no “FAQ” in the menu.
The whole page already covers all of that as one long flow.

⸻

PAGE STRUCTURE (one ultra-long page)

Below is the structure your landing page should follow, section by section.
This is what makes it feel like one long, modern, conversion-focused page.

⸻

1. HERO SECTION (Above the fold)
	•	Big promise (Bali-specific)
	•	Short subheading
	•	WhatsApp CTA
	•	3 quick trust badges
	•	Background image of a clean Bali pool

⸻

2. PROBLEM SECTION (Why Bali pools need pro care)
	•	Tropical algae
	•	Rainy season
	•	Pump failures
	•	Green water in 48 hours
	•	Tourist complaints / villa guests

Use bullets + short paragraphs.

⸻

3. SERVICE PACKAGES SECTION (3–4 cards)

Each service card should have:
	•	Title
	•	Short description
	•	What’s included
	•	“From IDR …” price anchor
	•	WhatsApp CTA

Packages:
	•	Weekly Maintenance
	•	Green Pool Rescue
	•	Hotel & Resort Service Contracts
	•	Equipment Repairs & Part Replacement

⸻

4. WHY CHOOSE US SECTION

Grid of benefits:
	•	Experienced Bali team
	•	Chemical management included
	•	Reports through WhatsApp
	•	Villa owners, hotels, resorts
	•	Fast response time
	•	Guaranteed clarity

⸻

5. BEFORE / AFTER RESULT SECTION

Side-by-side comparisons
	•	2–3 one-paragraph mini case stories.

⸻

6. SERVICE AREAS SECTION (Local SEO)

Mini-blocks for:
	•	Canggu
	•	Seminyak
	•	Sanur
	•	Ubud
	•	Denpasar
	•	Uluwatu
	•	Jimbaran
	•	Nusa Dua

Each with 2–3 lines and a local keyword.

⸻

7. HOW IT WORKS (3 simple steps)
	1.	WhatsApp us your location + pool photos
	2.	We visit & send price
	3.	Maintenance starts

Add icons, keep it clean.

⸻

8. TESTIMONIAL SECTION

3–6 reviews
With location tags like:
“Villa Owner – Canggu”
“Hotel Manager – Seminyak”

⸻

9. FAQ SECTION

Target long-tail questions:
	•	Why does my pool turn green in rainy season?
	•	How much is weekly maintenance in Bali?
	•	Do you include chemicals?
	•	Do you work with villa managers?
	•	Do you offer contracts for hotels?

Each answer: 3–5 lines.

⸻

10. FINAL CTA SECTION

Strong WhatsApp CTA
“Send us your location and pool photos — get a quote today.”

⸻

The footer is where ALL the “mini-pages” live.

Organize into 4 columns:

⸻

Column 1: Main Links
	•	Home
	•	Services
	•	Pool Cleaning
	•	Pool Maintenance
	•	Green Pool Cleaning
	•	Pool Repairs
	•	Chemical Management
	•	Hotel & Resort Contracts
	•	Emergency Pool Repair

⸻

Column 2: Service Areas
	•	Canggu
	•	Seminyak
	•	Uluwatu
	•	Jimbaran
	•	Sanur
	•	Ubud
	•	Denpasar
	•	Nusa Dua

Each of these is its own service-area landing page for SEO, but they don’t clutter the top menu.

⸻

Column 3: Company Info
	•	About Us
	•	Contact
	•	WhatsApp
	•	Opening Hours
	•	Terms & Conditions
	•	Privacy Policy

⸻

Column 4: Help & Info Pages
	•	FAQ
	•	Maintenance Checklist
	•	Pool Troubleshooting
	•	Guides (e.g. “How to fix green pool in Bali”)
	•	Chemical Safety
	•	Water Testing Standards
	•	Warranty & Guarantees

⸻

DESIGN FEEL (so it feels like ONE LONG PAGE)

To get the “longpage feeling”:
	•	White background
	•	Full-width sections
	•	Smooth gradient separators
	•	Sticky WhatsApp button bottom-right
	•	Consistent spacing (65–90px per block)
	•	Rounded-card style for services + testimonials
	•	No boxed layout — everything full width
	•	Light aqua + blue palette with bold CTA color (darker blue or teal)

⸻

WHY THIS WORKS

This structure:
	•	Gives Google all the SEO juice in the footer
	•	Keeps the top menu clean and premium
	•	Makes the page feel long, smooth, and modern
	•	Converts hard on mobile
	•	Feels like a real Bali service business
	•	Matches what expats + villa owners expect
